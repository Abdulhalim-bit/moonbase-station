01OVERVIEW

The Gateway
to the Cosmos

Founded in 2028 by a coalition of the world's leading aerospace organizations, Moonbase represents humanity's boldest leap into the cosmos. Our mission: establish a self-sustaining lunar colony that serves as the gateway to deep space exploration.

Located in the Shackleton Crater near the Moon's south pole, Moonbase Alpha leverages permanent sunlight on the crater rim for solar energy and access to water ice deposits in the permanently shadowed crater floor — making sustainable habitation possible for the first time.
